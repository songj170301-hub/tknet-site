Hyundai Group — AI Unicorn Investment Advisory

Investment Advisory ｜ Korea / Global

Summary:
Advised Hyundai Group on evaluating a high-growth AI company through comprehensive commercial, market, and technical due diligence, supporting a strategic minority investment decision.

Client Challenge:
Hyundai required clarity on the target’s market potential, competitive positioning, technology defensibility, valuation range, and downside risk exposure in a rapidly evolving AI landscape.

Our Approach:

  • Technology and product architecture due diligence
  • AI market TAM / SAM / SOM modeling

  • Financial modeling and valuation scenario analysis
  • Investment thesis and risk–return assessment

Impact:
Enabled informed investment decision-making with clearly articulated strategic fit, valuation logic, and risk–return trade-offs.
