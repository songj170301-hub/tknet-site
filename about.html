About TK Net

Since 2007 · Independent Strategic Advisory Seoul · Singapore · Tokyo · Beijing

Who We Are

TK Net is an independent advisory firm distinguished by global-standard analytical rigor, deep Asia expertise, and the ability to deliver large-scale innovation and investment initiatives from strategy through execution.

We advise chief executives, boards, investment committees, and public-sector leaders on decisions involving material capital, cross-border complexity, and long-term competitive consequence.

Evolution

2007 Founded as China Studies & Company, with close ties to Tsinghua University, Tsinghua Holdings, and leading global financial institutions.
2008–2009 Separated from Tsinghua Holdings to establish full operational and ownership independence. Began international expansion with presence in Singapore and Japan.
2009–2014 Established regional presence in Singapore together with IMC Group, Japan’s Mitsui, and Korea’s CKT Group.
2014 Rebranded as TK Net to reflect a broadened global scope across strategy, investment, and innovation-led development.
Today Independent strategic advisory partner to multinational corporations, investors, and governments across Asia, Europe, and North America.
