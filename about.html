About TK Net

Who We Are

TK Net is an independent advisory firm providing senior-level strategic counsel to corporations, investors, and public-sector leaders navigating complex, high-stakes decisions.

Our work spans corporate strategy, investment evaluation, innovation-led development, and cross-border transformation initiatives.

Evolution

2007 Founded as China Studies & Company, working closely with leading academic, financial, and institutional partners.
2008–2009 Established full independence and expanded advisory scope across Asia.
2009–2014 Built regional presence with partners including IMC Group, Mitsui, and CKT Group.
2014 Rebranded as TK Net, reflecting a global, multi-sector advisory mandate.
Today Trusted advisor on strategy, investment, and innovation initiatives across Asia, Europe, and North America.
