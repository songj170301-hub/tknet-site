Who We Are

TK Net is an independent advisory firm distinguished by global-standard analytical rigor, deep Asia expertise, and the rare ability to deliver large-scale innovation districts from strategy through activation.

We advise chief executives, boards, investment committees, and public-sector leaders on decisions involving material capital, cross-border complexity, and long-term competitive consequence.

Evolution

  • 2007: Founded as China Studies & Company, one of the first consulting firms in China with close ties to Tsinghua University, Tsinghua Holdings Co., Ltd., and leading global financial institutions.
  • 2008–2009: Separated from Tsinghua Holdings to establish full operational and ownership independence. Began international expansion with presence in Singapore and Japan.
  • 2009–2014: Presence established in Singapore together with IMC Group, Japan’s Mitsui, Korea’s CKT Group.
  • 2010: Formed strategic cooperation with CKT Group.
  • 2014: Rebranded as TK Net to reflect broadened global scope across strategy, investment, and large-scale innovation development.
  • Today: Independent strategic advisory partner to multinational corporations, investors, and governments across Asia, Europe, and North America.
